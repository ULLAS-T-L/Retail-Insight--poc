# Promo Execution Limits
## Context
While price drop tactics expand consumer capture securely, explicit barriers prevent prolonged hyper-inflation or margin devastation.

## Policy Rules
- If `promo_flag` is Active (1), `price_index` MUST safely drop strictly between 85 and 95 natively.
- Prolonged promotional conditions causing `price_index` beneath 85 signal strict margin destruction flags universally.
- Actions: Reverse active hyper-promotions back into 90+ standard index ranges to prevent margin collapse natively.
