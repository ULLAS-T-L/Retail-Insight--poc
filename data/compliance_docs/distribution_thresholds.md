# General Compliance: Distribution Thresholds
## Context
For brand presence stability, no authorized retailer is permitted to exhibit product distribution limits below strict minimal bounds without generating automatic strategic warning conditions.

## Policy Rules
- Any product metric for `distribution` falling below 60% within the past 12 weeks represents a critical violation of compliance strategy.
- Out of Stock conditions (OOS) must trigger immediate replenishment pipelines.
- Actions: Store operations must launch immediate inventory checks if distribution is discovered < 60%.
